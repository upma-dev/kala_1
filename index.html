Our Story

A Creative Community Born in Indore

The Kala Club is more than a workshop — it's a movement. We believe every person has a creative spark inside them, and all they need is the right space, the right community, and a little paint to let it out.

We started small, with a handful of curious souls in Indore who just wanted to try something new. Today, we host workshops on everything from resin art to mirror mosaics, bringing together artists, beginners, students, and families under one colourful roof.
